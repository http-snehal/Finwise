## What is FinWise?
FinWise is a gamified financial literacy platform built for India's first jobbers and fresh graduates. Instead of reading boring articles about money, you live through financial stories, complete quests, and run real-world simulations — all while earning XP, badges and streaks.
Think Duolingo, but for your salary, taxes and investments.

## The Problem We're Solving
Every year, millions of Indian graduates receive their first salary and have no idea what happened to it. TDS, EPF, Professional Tax — nobody taught them this. FinWise fixes that.

## How It Works
FinWise is structured as modules, each with 3 stages:

- **Story** — A short, cinematic dialogue scene sets the context
- **Quest** — Interactive learning through discovery and conversation
- **Simulation** — Apply what you learned with virtual money, zero real risk


## Current Modules

- **Module 1: The Paycheck** — Understand your CTC, TDS, EPF and take-home salary
- **Module 2: The Investor** — Learn investing basics, beat inflation, build your first portfolio
- **Module 3: The Tax Shield** — learn more about tax saving and tax planning also about GST
- **Module 4: The GST Forge** — Coming soon


## Characters

- **Laxmi** — Your warm financial mentor, present across all modules
- **Tax-Man Tanmay** — The charming villain who teaches you to legally pay less tax
- **Inflation Iqbal** — Module 2's antagonist who silently eats your idle savings


## Gamification

- ⚡ **XP** earned on every lesson and correct response
- ❤️ **Hearts** — limited lives that create real stakes
- 🔥 **Streaks** — daily habit building
- 🏆 **Badges** — milestone rewards like "Payslip Pro" and "First Investor"
- 🗺️ **Skill Tree** — visual progress map across all modules
